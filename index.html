Frequently Asked Questions

01.) What areas do you service?

Shockwave Electric serves Sarasota County, Hillsborough County, Port Charlotte, DeSoto County, and Hardee County. Andrew drives throughout Southwest Florida for residential and light commercial electrical work. Note that there is a small radius within Sarasota County where service is unavailable, but Andrew is happy to refer you to a trusted contact for those locations.

02.) Do I need a permit for my electrical project?

Most panel replacements, new circuits, and EV charger installations require a permit. The good news is Andrew handles all of that for you. He pulls the permit, schedules the inspection, and coordinates the FPL disconnect and reconnect when needed. You do not have to navigate any of it on your own.

03.) How does pricing and payment work?

Every project starts with a free estimate. All work requires a 30% deposit to book your job. Electrical pricing varies depending on the scope, materials, and scheduling. Andrew gives you a clear quote before any work begins so there are no surprises when the job is done.

04.) Do you work on commercial properties?

Yes. Shockwave Electric handles light commercial electrical work including service calls, commercial lighting retrofits, EV charging stations, and panel work for banks, retail spaces, offices, and multifamily properties. Andrew focuses on light commercial and does not take on heavy industrial or restaurant work.

05.) Will the same electrician show up every time?

Yes. Andrew is the owner and the electrician. He is not going to send a different crew to your home or business. When you hire Shockwave Electric, you work directly with the licensed professional who quoted your job from start to finish.
